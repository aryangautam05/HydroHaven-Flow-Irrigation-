Cookie Policy

At HydroHaven Flow Irrigation, we value your privacy and transparency. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website to enhance your experience and provide tailored services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device by your web browser when you visit websites. They are widely used to make websites work or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Cookies help us remember your preferences, enable certain features, and collect anonymous data about website usage for improvement purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

On our website, we use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of the website. They enable core functionalities such as security, authentication, and accessibility. Without these, the website cannot function properly.
  • Performance Cookies: These collect information about how visitors use the website, such as which pages are most visited. This data helps us improve the website's functionality and user experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember choices you make, like your language or region, to provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. They may also limit the number of times you see an ad.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies allow us to:

  • Recognize your device and remember your preferences during and between visits.
  • Customize content and tailor our communications to meet your needs.
  • Analyze website traffic and user interactions to continuously improve our site.
  • Enhance the security of our website and protect you from fraud.
  • Deliver targeted advertising that you are more likely to find helpful.

By understanding how you use our website, we can make informed improvements and ensure a better experience for all visitors.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have full control over the cookies stored on your device, and you can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies, but please be aware that disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our website, limiting functionality or personalization.

Below are some general instructions to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Go to Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Go to Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies and site data.
  • Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data.

You may also use browser extensions or tools to block or filter cookies according to your preferences.

Cookies from Third Parties

Some cookies used on our website may be set by third-party services such as analytics providers, advertising partners, or social media platforms. These cookies help these third parties deliver, analyze, and optimize their services via our site.

We do not control these third-party cookies directly. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these external providers to understand their cookie practices.

Privacy and Security Implications

Cookies themselves do not collect personal information about you directly. However, combined with other data, they may be used to identify or profile your interests and preferences.

At HydroHaven, protecting your privacy is paramount. We ensure that all data collected through cookies is stored securely, handled responsibly, and only used to enhance your experience and our services.

You can always opt to decline cookies using the consent dialog below or manage settings via your browser.
